# Welcome.

You've found the Archive. This is the part of Dad Humor where we slow down a bit. The main app is for one joke at a time, tap, react, move on. This is for everything that doesn't fit in that loop.

## What you'll find here

- **Data stories.** What happens when you run 50,000 reactions through a spreadsheet.
- **Guides.** Like "how to write a dad joke that lands" or "the anatomy of the perfect groan."
- **Press.** When journalists ask us things, we put the long version here.
- **Brand notes.** Why everything is yellow. Why Stache has nine moods. Why we don't take ourselves seriously, but we do take the jokes seriously.

## What you won't find here

A newsletter sign-up popup that takes over the screen. Auto-playing video. A list of "27 dad jokes that will make you ROFL" with each one on a separate page so we can serve more ads. We are not that website.

We are this website. Welcome.
