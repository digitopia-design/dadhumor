Why this works better

✓ Two-font system mirrors comedic delivery. Setup feels conversational (Inter), punchline lands hard (Outfit). Same way a comedian actually performs.

✓ Outfit is more refined than Space Grotesk. Less "design school," more confident. Holds up better at huge sizes for OG share cards.

✓ White-at-70% beats grey for muted text. Always WCAG AAA. Visually softer, structurally accessible. No more "is this readable?" anxiety.

✓ Pink and Red bumped to AA pass. Tiny adjustments, no visible difference, full accessibility win.

✓ Setup at weight 400 lets the punchline breathe. Hierarchy comes from font choice + weight + size, not just colour. Three layers of contrast.
